Welcome to EventHub. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how EventHub ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our platform for booking comedy shows, movies, events, and live concerts.

By using EventHub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We periodically update this policy, and we will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Billing and payment information
  • Event preferences and booking history
  • Communication preferences

Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Cookies and tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

Event Booking & Management

Process your ticket purchases and manage event registrations.

Personalized Recommendations

Suggest events based on your preferences and booking history.

Security & Fraud Prevention

Protect your account and prevent fraudulent activities.

Communication

Send booking confirmations, updates, and promotional offers.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We respect your privacy and only share your information in the following circumstances:

Event Organizers

We share necessary information with event organizers to facilitate your ticket entry and event experience.

Payment Processors

Your payment information is processed by secure third-party payment providers.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
