Refund Policy

Our policies regarding refunds, cancellations, and ticket transfers for EventHub events.

General Refund Policy

At EventHub, we strive to provide the best experience for our users. Our refund policy is designed to be fair to both event organizers and attendees.

Most refund requests must be submitted at least 48 hours before the event start time.

Refund Eligibility

  • Refunds are available for canceled events
  • Refunds may be available if an event is rescheduled and you cannot attend the new date
  • Refunds are available if the event description is significantly different from the actual event
  • Refunds are available if you can't attend due to certain emergency situations (documentation may be required)

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind or personal schedule conflicts
  • Failure to attend the event without prior cancellation
  • Partial attendance of an event
  • Events specifically marked as "No Refund" at the time of purchase

Refund Processing Time

Once your refund is approved, it may take 5-10 business days for the funds to appear in your account, depending on your payment method and financial institution.

Credit/Debit Cards

5-7 business days

PayPal

3-5 business days

EventHub Wallet

24-48 hours

How to Request a Refund

  1. Log in to your EventHub account

    Go to the EventHub website and sign in to your account.

  2. Navigate to "My Tickets"

    Find the section that shows your upcoming events and tickets.

  3. Select the event

    Click on the event for which you want to request a refund.

  4. Click "Request Refund"

    Follow the prompts to complete your refund request.

  5. Wait for confirmation

    You'll receive an email confirmation once your request is processed.

Event Cancellations

If an event is canceled by the organizer, you will automatically receive a full refund to your original payment method. No action is required on your part.

In case of event cancellation, refunds are processed within 10 business days.

Need Help?

If you have questions about our refund policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact our support team.
