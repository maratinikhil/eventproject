Frequently Asked Questions

Booking tickets on EventHub is simple:

  1. Find the event you want to attend
  2. Select the number of tickets and preferred seating
  3. Click "Book Now" and proceed to checkout
  4. Enter your payment information and confirm booking
  5. You'll receive an email confirmation with your e-tickets

Our cancellation policy depends on the event organizer:

  • Most comedy shows allow cancellations up to 24 hours before the event
  • Movie tickets are generally non-refundable
  • Concert tickets may have specific refund windows
  • Check the event details page for specific cancellation policies

You can contact event organizers through several methods:

  • Visit the event page and click "Contact Organizer"
  • Use the messaging system in your EventHub account
  • Find contact information in your booking confirmation email
  • For urgent issues, contact our support team who can assist
